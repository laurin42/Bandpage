We are
Burnheart Mockery

Burnheart Mockery is a hard rock band consisting of five talented musicians who have been playing together for several years. The band was originally formed in the basement of a commune, where they took their first musical steps.

With their handcrafted hard rock music, they quickly made a name for themselves in the local music scene and gathered a loyal following. Their energetic live performances and captivating songs consistently thrill their audience.

Fueled by their passion for loud guitars, powerful drums, and catchy melodies, Burnheart Mockery has created a sound of their own that sets them apart from other bands. Inspired by the greats of hard rock from the past, they infuse their own modern twist into the music while remaining authentic and sincere.

Burnheart Mockery is more than just a band - they are a community of musicians who share a passion for rock music and embark on an unforgettable musical journey with their fans. With every song they write and every performance they play, they continue their mission to rock the world with their music and spread their message of freedom and passion.
